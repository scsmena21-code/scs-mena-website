SERVICES

HUMINT-led services for due diligence and disputes.

Structured around buyer needs: when to use each service, what we do, what you receive, and how quickly we can deliver.

Due Diligence (HUMINT-led)

When clients use this

Pre-investment checks on counterparties and beneficial owners.

Third-party onboarding where integrity risk is unclear.

Board-level decisions where open-source records are incomplete.

What we do

We combine desk research with local-source HUMINT and targeted records retrieval to verify identity, control, litigation exposure, and reputational signals.

What you receive

  • Concise risk narrative with clear findings and implications
  • Source-backed evidence notes and verification trail
  • Priority flags and practical next-step recommendations

Typical timeline: 3-10 working days depending on scope and jurisdictions.

Common add-ons: Court filing retrieval, local narrative mapping, CV and credential verification.

Reputational Intelligence

When clients use this

Early warning on reputational exposure before engagement.

Disputes and sensitive transactions requiring narrative context.

Regulatory or stakeholder scrutiny in high-context markets.

What we do

We map adverse media, local narratives, and source-based sentiment to separate noise from material integrity risk.

What you receive

  • Structured reputational risk profile by issue type
  • Timeline of notable incidents and narrative shifts
  • Plain-language assessment of business impact

Typical timeline: 2-7 working days based on subject profile and depth.

Common add-ons: Executive profiling, competitor context, escalation watchlist.

Registry and Court Filings Research

When clients use this

When registry/court data is not fully available online.

When ownership, litigation, or filing status needs direct verification.

When legal teams need records-ready support for active matters.

What we do

We retrieve and interpret available local records through in-country channels, then cross-verify against open-source and client-provided data.

What you receive

  • Retrieved filing extracts and clear record summaries
  • Entity and control-chain validation notes
  • Documented gaps, limitations, and confidence levels

Typical timeline: 3-12 working days depending on jurisdiction access and volume.

Common add-ons: Translation support, litigation chronology, local counsel coordination.

Investigations and Strategic Intelligence

When clients use this

Disputes, asset-trace leads, and complex cross-border risk events.

Situations requiring deeper source development and iterative inquiry.

Cases where standard DD outputs are not sufficient for decision-making.

What we do

We run targeted investigative workstreams using HUMINT, records research, and pattern analysis to uncover relevant hidden links and risk drivers.

What you receive

  • Action-oriented intelligence briefings and progress updates
  • Evidence-backed hypothesis testing and lead development
  • Final report with findings, constraints, and decision options

Typical timeline: Rapid scopes from 2-5 days; extended investigations over 2-4+ weeks.

Common add-ons: Fixed-scope rapid trace, deeper jurisdiction expansion, ongoing monitoring retainers.
